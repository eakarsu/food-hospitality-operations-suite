## Recipe Menu Planning

- `recipe-builder` - Recipe Builder: Ingredients, instructions, portions, prep timing, substitutions, and kitchen notes.
- `menu-digitization` - Menu Digitization: Menu photos, extracted items, categories, prices, descriptions, and cleanup queue.
- `food-costing` - Food Costing: Ingredient costs, portion margins, vendor changes, and menu profitability.
- `nutrition-allergen-review` - Nutrition & Allergen Review: Nutrition estimates, allergen flags, dietary tags, and compliance notes.
